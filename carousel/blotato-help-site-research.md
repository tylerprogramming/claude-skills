## What is Blotato

Blotato is an "all-in-one AI content workspace to create, remix, and distribute viral posts and faceless videos." Built by Sabrina Ramonov, who grew a personal brand to over 1.3 million followers within 15 months WITHOUT a team, budget, VA, paid ads, masterminds, courses, coaches, consultants, agencies, prior existing audience, or prior social media experience.

**Core capabilities:**
- Content remixing (transform videos to social posts, articles into scripts)
- AI-generated media (faceless images, videos, voice content)
- Multi-platform publishing (schedule and publish across 9 channels simultaneously)
- Trend monitoring (identifies viral content patterns)
- Visual generation (carousels, infographics, quote cards, AI videos)
- Inspiration database (curated viral posts for remixing)
- Viral AI Coach (scores and improves short-form video hooks)
- Brand Kit (maintains consistent brand voice and visuals)

**Target users:** Solopreneurs, content creators, small business owners, social media marketers, digital marketing agencies.

## Content Creation Strategy & Philosophy

### What Audiences Actually Want
1. Solutions to problems (not just content formats)
2. Outcome framing over feature description
   - Bad: "Here's a video about my product"
   - Better: "Here's how I saved 10 hours weekly using this AI tool"
3. Problem-solving, curiosity satisfaction, or inspiration

### Format vs. Solution
A format (video, carousel, thread) is the delivery mechanism. The solution addresses pain points or inspires action. Audiences don't care about the container; they care about the outcome.

### Key Principles
- **"Do, Then Share"** - Document results through execution rather than theorizing
- **Simple systems scale** - Complex systems break down
- **Product over marketing** for SaaS founders - 80% product, 20% marketing
- **Execution over strategy** - Increase effort rather than constantly pivoting
- **Content as credibility proof** - Share concrete results to stand out
- **Audience ownership** - Email newsletter is the sole owned channel

### Making AI Output Sound Like You
1. **Import custom prompts** - Centralized prompt management
2. **Personalize existing prompts** by editing three core areas:
   - Example 1 and Example 2 (replace with sample posts you like)
   - Guidelines section (add audience info, products, keywords, desired flow)
3. Customize writing style elements: sentence structure, voice (active preferred), formatting, tone markers, prohibited words, punctuation

### Managing Multiple Brand Voices
1. Create separate prompts for each brand personality
2. Provide platform-specific examples and guidelines
3. Apply appropriate prompts when creating content
4. Regular maintenance and adjustment
5. Configure Brand Kit for visual personalization

## Platform-Specific Strategies

### LinkedIn
- **Max daily posts:** 2
- Post during morning hours; repost in early evening
- Under 10k followers: 30-60 min daily commenting on peers' posts
- Minimum 3 posts/week when starting; daily if quality maintained
- Strong engagement in first 1-2 hours drives significantly greater reach
- Space out text - avoid large blocks
- Storytelling with personal stories, failures, data points
- AVOID: copycat content, over-promotion, posting and ghosting, neglecting profile
- Currently has "terrible bot and AI slop problem"
- Slowest platform to grow on, but worth it

### Twitter/X
- **Max daily posts:** 5 (under 10k); 15 (over 10k)
- Under 10k: prioritize engagement over content creation
- Threads supported via Blotato (auto-handles reply chaining)
- Can cross-post threads to Bluesky and Threads

### TikTok
- **Max daily posts:** 5 videos
- Under 50k followers: post frequently with 1-2 hour intervals
- **4-week warmup for new accounts** (scroll, comment, bookmark like regular user)
- Only post after consistently getting 200+ views
- Use existing account if possible (hide old videos via privacy, don't delete)
- Film on TikTok native app with front camera, no external mic needed
- Keep initial videos to 30 seconds max
- Get first 100 videos out ASAP
- Use Creator Search Insights for keyword research (200k+ searches minimum)
- Keywords in title, description, and captions
- 35% of TikTok traffic comes from search - SEO-optimize captions

### Instagram
- **Max daily posts:** 1 image post or 4 reels
- Prioritize Reels - Instagram actively promotes them
- Polished videos perform better than on TikTok
- Carousels increasingly promoted
- Collaborators supported (enter handle without @)
- Custom cover images via URL in advanced options

### Facebook
- **Max daily posts:** 4 reels
- Creator profiles: manual posting only
- Pages: automation and Business Manager access
- "Posted by Blotato" label appears (platform transparency policy, cannot be removed)

### Pinterest
- **Max daily posts:** 30 pins
- Use trends.pinterest.com for keywords under 1M monthly searches
- Create 20-30 SEO-optimized boards initially
- Pin 20 others' pins per board first to teach algorithm

### YouTube
- **Max daily posts:** 1 long video or 4 shorts
- For camera-shy: consider video podcast format with guest interviews
- Distribute long-form to Spotify and Apple Music

### 2nd Account Strategy (TikTok)
Create a secondary account dedicated to curating niche-specific viral content. Use it to study successful videos and identify trends without algorithm distraction. Maintain separate accounts for different content categories.

## Hook Frameworks

"The hook is the most important piece of your content, whether text or video." First 5 seconds determine everything.

### 10 Hook Frameworks

1. **Pattern Interrupt** - Surprising visual + unexpected stat + solution + evidence
2. **Authority Builder** - Common belief + counter data + better approach + outcomes
3. **Problem Agitate Solution** - Identify problem + intensify + unveil solution + transformation
4. **Hidden Secret** - Hint at discovery + spark curiosity + disclose method + application
5. **Before/After Bridge** - Initial state + suggest transformation + connect with solution + validate
6. **Educational Hook** - Engaging question + valuable info + solution + call to action
7. **Social Proof** - Results upfront + credibility + explain method + next steps
8. **Myth Buster** - Common myth + refute + present truth + evidence
9. **Quick Win** - Promise immediate result + simple method + emphasize ease + confirm speed
10. **FOMO** - Highlight opportunity + urgency + what might be missed + solution

### Hook Components for Video
- First 4 sentences you say
- Video title
- Visual hook (images, green screen effects)

### Brainstorming with Blotato
Navigate to Remix > Load prompt from library > "Brainstorm Tiktok IG Video Hooks" > Generate ideas

## Blotato Features & Tools

### Sources (Content Input)
Supported types: text, articles/websites, YouTube, TikTok, audio/podcasts/meetings, PDFs, Perplexity AI, Twitter/X posts

- Sources stored locally in browser (NOT synced across devices)
- Custom instructions can pre-process each source
- One high-quality source can generate numerous diverse posts
- "Garbage in, garbage out" - source quality determines output quality

### Post Creation
1. Add source material (URL, text, PDF, etc.)
2. Select target platforms (9 supported: Instagram, LinkedIn, Facebook, Threads, TikTok, Twitter, Bluesky, YouTube, Pinterest)
3. AI generates platform-specific drafts
4. Refine with integrated AI writer
5. Add media (upload or AI-generate)
6. Publish immediately, schedule, or use next free slot

### Viral AI Coach
- Analyzes short-form video drafts against thousands of viral videos
- Delivers "brutally honest and specific actionable feedback"
- Scorecard: opening hook, pain/benefit hook, visual comprehensibility, information density, emotional resonance
- Generates 10 suggested hooks from 1,000+ curated viral hooks database
- Recommends optimized hashtags (3 broad + 2 niche)
- Included in all paid plans, unlimited use, no credits consumed

### Inspiration Database
- Library of top-performing posts from Twitter creators
- Search by topics, creators, filters
- Shuffle for random discovery
- Remix posts directly into your own content
- Find influencers via "Creator" filter
- Request new topics

### AI Images
Three models: Realistic (Recraft), Best for Text (Ideogram), Creative (Flux)
- Auto-generates prompts from post content
- Editable "Magic Prompt" for manual control
- Known issue: AI sometimes misspells text in images
- Free tool at tools.sabrina.dev for experimenting with prompts

### AI Videos
- **AI Agent method:** Describe video in plain language, system produces branded result using Brand Kit
- **Template method:** More customization control (AI Video with AI Voice, Image Slideshow with Text Overlays)
- Recommended workflow: storyboard with cheap images first, then finalize with premium
- AI voiceover via ElevenLabs (speed controlled by punctuation/line breaks in script)
- Custom images, audio, and music can be uploaded
- Export as MP4 or publish directly to platforms

### Visual Templates
Categories: Image Slideshows, Quote Cards, Tweet Cards, Tutorial Carousels, Images with Text, Video Editor, AI Videos, AI Avatar, Legacy Infographics

### Content Calendar
- Slot-based scheduling across platforms
- Multi-platform support per slot
- Bulk operations for managing scheduled content
- Failed post tracking with error explanations

### Small Edits
Select text > describe desired change > press Enter. Handles: remove formatting, convert to bullet lists, expand ideas, etc.

### Multiple Drafts
Work on several drafts simultaneously with independent source material per draft.

### Language Support
90+ languages supported (all ChatGPT languages). Default language setting controls all generated text. For videos, write script/prompt in target language directly.

## API & Automation

### API Overview
- Base URL: https://backend.blotato.com/v2
- Auth: `blotato-api-key: YOUR_API_KEY` header
- Rate limits: 30 req/min (creation), 60 req/min (polling)
- All operations async: Submit - Poll - Result
- Limited to paying subscribers

### Core API Endpoints
- `GET /v2/users/me/accounts` - List connected accounts
- `POST /v2/posts` - Publish/schedule posts
- `GET /v2/posts/:postSubmissionId` - Check post status
- `POST /v2/source-resolutions-v3` - Create source (extract content)
- `GET /v2/source-resolutions-v3/:id` - Get source status
- `POST /v2/videos/from-templates` - Create visual
- `GET /v2/videos/creations/:id` - Get visual status
- `GET /v2/videos/templates` - List available templates

### MCP Server (Claude Code / Claude Desktop / Cursor)
URL: `https://mcp.blotato.com/mcp`
9 tools available:
1. `blotato_get_user` - Account info
2. `blotato_list_accounts` - Connected accounts
3. `blotato_create_post` - Publish/schedule across 9 platforms
4. `blotato_get_post_status` - Track post status
5. `blotato_extract_content` - Extract from URLs/text (8 source types)
6. `blotato_list_templates` - Available visual templates
7. `blotato_create_visual` - Generate visuals from templates
8. `blotato_get_visual_status` - Track generation status
9. `blotato_upload_media` - Upload media from public URL

### Claude Code Setup
```bash
claude mcp add blotato \
  --url https://mcp.blotato.com/mcp \
  --header "blotato-api-key: YOUR_API_KEY"
```

## Social Platform Technical Requirements

### Twitter/X
- 280 chars (standard) / 25,000 (Premium)
- Up to 4 photos, 1 GIF, or 1 video per tweet
- Images: JPG/PNG/GIF/WEBP, under 5MB
- Video: H.264, 30/60 FPS, up to 512MB, 0.5-140 seconds
- Rate: 100 requests/24 hours per user

### Instagram
- Caption: 2,200 chars
- Images: JPEG/PNG, max 8MB, 320-1440px wide, 4:5 to 1.91:1 ratio
- Video: MP4/MOV, max 100MB, 3 sec to 15 min (Reels), 4:5 to 1.91:1
- Carousel: 2-10 items, all crop to first image's aspect ratio

### LinkedIn
- 3,000 chars per post
- Images: JPG/GIF/PNG, max 5MB, recommended 1200x627px (1.91:1)
- Video: MP4 only, 75KB-500MB, 3 sec to 30 min, up to 4096x2304, 60 FPS
- Optimal square: 1080x1080

### Facebook
- 63,206 chars via Graph API
- Images: JPEG/PNG/GIF/BMP/TIFF, max 30MB, min 1080px wide
- Video: MP4/MOV, max 4GB, 3 sec to 240 min, up to 30 FPS

### TikTok
- Description: 2,200 chars
- Video: MP4 (recommended)/WebM/MOV, max 4GB, up to 10 min, 23-60 FPS, min 360px
- Images: WebP/JPEG only (PNG must convert), max 20MB, max 1080px

### Pinterest
- Description: 800 chars, Title: 100 chars
- Images: PNG/JPEG, max 20MB
- Video: MP4/MOV, 4 sec to 5 min, max 2GB
- Carousel: 2-5 images

### Threads
- 500 chars
- Images: JPEG/PNG, max 8MB, 320-1440px
- Video: MP4/MOV, 1 sec to 5 min, max 1GB, up to 1920px wide
- Carousel: 2-20 items

### Bluesky
- 300 chars
- Up to 4 images per post
- Square: 1080x1080, Landscape: 1200x627, Portrait: 627x1200

### YouTube
- Title: 100 chars, Description: 5,000 chars
- Video: MP4 recommended (H.264 + AAC-LC), up to 256GB or 12 hours
- Thumbnail: 1280x720, 16:9, max 2MB
- Banner: 2560x1440, max 6MB
